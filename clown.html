The Clowns of Your Nightmare



Description


This is a Custom map made for 1 until 6 players.
You play as a hero which have 2 basics spells and 4 upgradable ones through points you earn each level reached.

Context

The Kirin Tor's Archmages thought to have find a way to purify zombies and contaminated grains but when they test it, zombies turn into mad clowns. Stop them if not Lordaeron will be surrounded and fall once and for all.

Single Player Gameplay


History mode


You incarnate a Paladin who wants to purify the city against mad clowns following a Kirin Tor's mistake.

The playground is divided on 3 parts each one divided by a forcefield. Each forcefield is desactivated when both conditions are true :
  • Destroy the factory of the area
  • Kill all the clowns of the area
While a factory is alive, a clown will spawn each 10 seconds at the factory location.

Quests :
  • Destroy the three Clown factories
  • Kill Arthas the clown
  • Eradicate Clowns
  • Destroy the three Clown factories again
  • Kill Kael the Joker
  • Destroy the last three Clown factories
  • Kill the two bosses
  • Eradicate the last Clowns

Massacre Circus mode
Your Paladin have been catch by the Arthas' Gang and they want to kill you during the Massacre Circus Show

The playground is a small area where clowns appears. Each round the number of clowns who will spawn increases but each 5 rounds clowns are stronger but their number is reset to 1.

Quest : Win the 48 rounds.

MultiPlayer Gameplay


History mode


A Team of 3 players play in the Paladin's side and the other one play in the Arthas' Gang side
Each player have 3 lives, A player who reach 0 lose

Each time a clown factory is spawned, each hero in the Arthas' Gang level up and the Paladin's side ones level down
Each time a clown factory is destroyed, each hero in the Paladin's side level up and the Arthas' Gang ones level down

Use the marketplace to have more chance to win
Item sold in the marketplace
Item effect Increases your lives of 1 when you use it. Remove cooldown of all your spells. Steal a life to your opponent when you launch a spell to him. Improves your hero's abilities. Prevent units in the target area to cast spells. Automatically brings the Hero back to life with 500 hit points when the Hero wearing the Ankh dies. Activate Avatar to temporarily give the owner 5 bonus armor, 500 bonus hit points, 20 bonus damage and spell immunity.

Slaughterhouse of Clowns


Select your hero and survive until the other team dies.
Each 5 levels, clowns become stronger
Cast spells to the other player to puzzle him or to increase your lucky.

Quest : Survive as long as possible
