Worker Resistance



Description


This is a Custom map made for 4 players
Waves of Enraged rabbits comes to your position to eradicate the city and those who are in their paths. To defend their native land, A group of worker organise themselves to force these beasts to pullback and prevent them to come back.

Context


A city of Lordaeron is threaten by attempt escapes of enraged rabbits which are more and more frequently and more and more difficult to stop.
Make a defence with your teammates, Find the Spawns spots and kick rabbits' ass.

Gameplay


Workers


All the classic races are playable

Human

Units
Basic worker unit, Repair structures. Can become Militia. Attacks land units.
A powerful mounted warrior. Can learn Animal War Training, use Inner Fire and Devotion Aura. Attacks land units.
An ability caster. He can learn Fire Bolt and Fire Rain. Attacks land and air units.
A horse who can stock items and put down Goblin Mine. Doesn't Attack.
Heavily armored vehicule, effective to tank a large enemy troop. Attacks land units.
A worker unit specialised against caster units. Is Immune to magic, has Feedback and can build an Arcane Tower. Attacks land units.

Orc

Units
Basic worker unit, Use Repair. Can also go inside Orc Burrows to give it an attack. Become corrupt if Orcish Rage is research. Attacks land units.
A strong mounted unit. Have Bloodlust and Command Aura.
The only one orc caster-type unit. He can learn Forked Lightning and Purge. Attacks land and air units.
A resistant beast use to slow an enemy assault. It can use Devour and Slam. Doesn't Attack.
Long-range siege weaponry. Effective against building but slow and vulnerable. Can learn the Burning Oil ability. Attacks land units and trees.
A mystic unit who use wards to defend himself and to eradicate magic energies. Cannot Attack

Undead

Units
Basic worker. Can Restore structures. To protect himself he can become Angry. Attacks land units.
A powerful melee unit. it can learn Disease Cloud Aura and Cannibalize. Attacks land units.
Your spell-caster. He can initially cast Virus which turn an alive unit to a Zombie when he die. He can also learn Death Coil and Animate Dead. Attacks land and air units.
A tactical air unit. Can teleport entire armies with Dark summoning.
An invisible spirit who can detect other invisible units. Can use Shackles and Dispel magic but cannot attack.

Night Elf

Units
Basic worker unit. Renew Night elf buildings. With BOOM, they can inflict damage to units around them.
A spirit of night elves come back to life to revenge itself. It can use Spell Steal, Cyclone and Werewolf. Attacks land and air units.
A Demon Hunter caged use his power to help you in exchange to his freedom. Can use Manaburn, Immolation and Evasion.
The Tree of Life is a powerful ancient able to cast War Stomp, Force of Nature and Breath of Ice. Attacks land units.
A demigod is an Keeper Of The Grove falls in battle. They have Resurrection, Divine Shield and Reincarnation but don't stand a long time. Attacks land and air units.

3 Difficulties can be choosen :
  • Easy : Preparation time :10 min, Food Supply :50, Spawn spot to block : 1
  • Intermediate : Preparation time :7 min 30 sec, Food Supply :75, Spawn spots to block : 2
  • Hard : Preparation time : 5 min, Food Supply :100, 3 Spawn spots to block : 3
Prepare to defend yourself from Endless waves of Enraged rabbits during the "Preparation time"
Your hero is the key to build structures.
Structures and Upgrades doesn't need ressources.
Units and the Hero cost food

Several Hostile Heroes can be find, they give an additionnal structure to your hero when you defeat them.

Give a Generator which can posses rabbits when it is activate
Give a Vampiric Sanctuary which can heal friendly units
Give a Pocket Factory which can launch Nuclear Strike.
Give a Rabbits' Cage which can give rabbits harmless


Quest : Block the spawn spot(s)

Cheat Codes


Golbut : 1 Giant abomination named Golbut spawn
Sheep : A sheep rain appear (rewrite the code to stop it)
Buffet : Set the food limit to the maximum
Deusexmachina : Set the handicap of the Enraged Rabbits to 50%

To use a cheat code type the code as a message :
  • Press Enter
  • Type the cheat code (ex: Sheep)
  • Press Enter again
A message appear where a cheat code is activated.
