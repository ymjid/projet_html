What's Warcraft III?

Menu







This is a Real Time Strategy video game released by Blizzard Entertainment in 2002, for PC and Mac.
The main particularity of this game compared to the predecessors games is the apparition of Heroes.
Heroes give a new dimension of Strategy : They can reverse the proceeding of a battle with their powerful abilities

Gameplay

The gameplay of the game have different phases :
Build an healthy economy
Find your opponent
Kill hostile/opponent's units with your hero to give him experience points.
Train an army to counter the opponent one

The aim of the game is to destroy all enemy buildings

More informations about the basics of the gameplay

How to play




Several game modes are available : 1v1 2v2 3v3 4v4 FFA
Four races are playable :
  • Human
  • Orc
  • Night Elf
  • Undead

Campaign Story



There are 5 Campaigns :
  • Exodus of the Horde (Prologue/Tutorial)
The orc warchief Thrall prepare the Horde to flee from Lordaeron to go to Kalimdor

  • The Scourge of Lordaeron (Human Campaign)
Prince Arthas investigate to the plague which ravaged north lands of Lordaeron and attempt to erradicate it

  • Path of the Damned (Undead Campaign)
Undead regroup the Cult of the damned and want to bring Kel'Thuzad to life.

  • The Invasion of Kalimdor (Orc Campaign)
The Horde reach Kalimdor and research a Oracle to discover their fate.

  • Eternity's End (Night elf Campaign)
The Sentinel have learned The Burning Legion is back to Azeroth so they rush themselves to wake the druids up before it's too late.
This is the expansion set of Warcraft III - Reign of Chaos released in 2003
It add a new hero, 2 units and 1 building for each races.
The expansion give the possibilities to create and play Custom Campaigns, to Hire Neutral heroes at the Tavern and other stuff.

Campaign Story



There are 4 Campaigns :
  • Terror of the Tides
The Warden Maiev hunt the Demon Illidan for his sins and bring him back to jail

  • Curse of the Blood Elves
The Prince Kael'thas and the Blood Elves are condamned to death next to their alliance with the Naga

  • Legacy of the Damned
The Frozen Throne is damaged and in danger, the Death Knight loses his power and must come back to Northrend to protect it.

  • The Founding of Durotar
The Beastmaster Rexxar join the Horde to help them to found the Fortress of Durotar in the continent of Kalimdor

System Requirements*

Windows

  • Operating System : Windows XP / Windows Vista
  • Processor : 400 MHz Pentium II or equivalent
  • Video : 8 MB 3D video card (TNT, i810, Voodoo 3, Rage 128 equivalent or better) with DirectX® 8.1 support
  • Memory : 128 MB RAM
  • Storage : 700 MB available HD space
  • Internet : Multiplayer requires a low-latency, active Internet connection rated at 28.8 KBps+. LAN play requires a TCP/IP connection.
  • Media : 4X CD-ROM drive

Mac

  • Operating System : Macintosh® OS X 10.3.9 to 10.6.8
  • Processor : 400 MHz G3 processor
  • Video : 16 MB ATI Technologies or Nvidia chipset 3D video card
  • Memory : 128 MB RAM
  • Storage : 700 MB available HD space
  • Internet : Multiplayer requires a low-latency, active Internet connection rated at 28.8 KBps+. LAN play requires a TCP/IP connection.
  • Media : 4X CD-ROM drive

  • Create your own units, Heroes and Abilities
